About CSI DDU Student branch:

CSI DDU student branch is a commity of 20 student of computer and IT Department,who organizes event related to computer in the Colladge.They help students to enhance their knowladge of computer by oraganizing workshops and seminar of the subject which is not covered in their academic syllabus.

CSI committe also help those student financially who wants to go for research but can't afford by giving them a scholarship.

You can be a member of CSI DDU Student branch committe in second year of your academic journey.

To became a part of this commity you should pass the entrance test taken by commity every year.This Entrance test is devided into two rounds.First is pen paper test and second is personal interview.Thus they tests participant on the basis of their technical and analytical knowledge.The student who is able to clear both the round gets this amazing chance to become a committe member of CSI.
